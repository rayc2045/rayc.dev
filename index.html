The Meaning of Design

The term design traces its origins to the Latin word designare, meaning "idea" or "plan". At its essence, design can be defined as the intentional act of creation. For designer Feng Yu, design is the process of uncovering and generating value. It revolves around three core principles: communicating with clarity, solving problems effectively, and achieving a balance between form and function.

Speaking of Programming

When a problem is identified, deliberate thought leads to a solution that can ultimately be expressed in a language a computer understands—this is programming. The rapid advancement of web technology is driven by our relentless pursuit to redefine and reevaluate problems. Programming not only imparts technical expertise but also fosters critical and creative thinking.
