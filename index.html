The Meaning of Design

The word design is derived from the Latin "Designare", which means idea or plan. The simplest definition of design is a purposeful creative act. Especially for designer Feng Yu, designing is to discover and create value. Three things: "Speaking clearly", "solving problems", and "matching functionality" can be said the most common use in design.

Speaking of Programming

When a problem is found, after thinking, the idea of solving the problem is finally expressed in a way that the computer can understand, that is, programming. The reason web technology develops so fast is that we redefine the problems over and over again. Learning programming teaches us how to think.
